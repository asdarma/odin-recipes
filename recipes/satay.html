Description

Satay is Indonesian style grilled meat on a skewer and served with peanut dipping sauce

Satay is considered one of the best Indonesian culinary and must try!

Ingredients

  • 500 gr Beef or Chicken, cut into 1-inch chunks
  • 1 tbsp Ground coriander
  • 100 gr Ground peanuts
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 2 cloves Onion
  • 1 tbsp Brown sugar
  • 1/2 tbsp Pepper
  • 1/2 tbsp Salt
  • 3 pcs Candlenut

Instruction to cook

  1. Marinate the meat with ground coriander for 15 minutes
  2. Fry the ground peanuts and mix with the other spices
  3. Put the spices on food processor and simmer on low heat until aromatic
  4. Prepare the grill, put meat on skewer and grill until cooked
  5. Dish with steamed rice and enjoy!
