Description

Rendang is a rich and tender coconut beef stew which is explosively flavourful. This dish originate from Minangkabau ethnic in Indonesia.

This dish often served at ceremonial occasions and to honor guests.

Ingredients

  • 500 gr Beef short ribs, cut into cubes
  • 3 pcs Cloves
  • 3 pcs Star Annise
  • 3 pods Cardamom
  • 1 cup Coconut milk
  • 2 tsp Tamarind pulp
  • 1 tbsp Palm sugar

Instruction to cook

  1. Chop all the spices and blend in food processor
  2. Heat oil in stew pot add the spices and stir-fry until aromatic
  3. Add the beef and Coconut milk and simmer on medium heat, stirring frequently until meat is cooked
  4. Once the meat is cooked, add the Palm sugar and continue to simmer on low heat until the meat is tender and gravy has dried up
  5. Serve immediately with steamed rice or save some for tommorow. Enjoy!
